ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Lala. I'm 26 years old and I'm an architect, graphic designer, and a visual artist based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. I've graduated from the University of Buenos Aires in 2017 with an architectural degree and I've been working on several art projects in the past few years. I'm interested in artistic expressions, tattoos and video games are my biggest passions. I'm currently studying a Master in Art Curatorship, I've always been intrigued by art theory, but I've always felt that the academic field draws a line between what can be considered art and what can't. In my work I try to bend that limit by creating an artistic experience, even if it's a small project. I'm a very passionate and ambitious person.

I'm a strong team worker, I've had the chance to work with a group of more than 50 people at interdisciplinary tasks, urbanizing informal and poor neighborhoods. This gave me the possibility to learn a lot from my coworkers. I value kindness and trust in my environment, I care for people and that's why I try to do the best job I can at every opportunity.

WHAT I'M WORKING ON

I'm currently tattooing in a studio based in Buenos Aires, along with many great artists. Nowadays, I'm working on large scale projects, trying to make collaborative designs by including the perspective of my client in every step of the creative process. Body movement is fundamental to me, so my work encompasses the idea of distortion and motion. Also, I've been working on different canvases and technics, doing some painting and digital illustration. For my next project coming up I hope to blend some of my artworks into a video game I'm starting to craft.

WHAT I'M INTERESTED IN

I'm driven by the idea of creating worlds. I think video games are the ultimate artistic expression. I'm particularly fascinated by the proccess of creating aesthetics. Designing an entirely world for the game to develop, from the colors, the textures and the general atmosphere, to the smallest details and even the perfect typography. and the The user interface and the user experience aesthetic appeling and touching. I've been creating assets for video games for a couple of years now, and this year at Global Game Jam (2020) I had the chance to create the art of a video game from scratch in just a few days. It was an amazing experience. I think my prolific and interdisciplinary path in design and art studies makes me perfect for a job in video games. I'm especially interested in working in art direction.
